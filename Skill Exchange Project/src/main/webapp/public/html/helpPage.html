About SkillExchange

SkillExchange is a peer-to-peer learning platform where users can exchange skills without monetary transactions. Our mission is to make knowledge sharing accessible to everyone.

Core Principles:

  • Teach What You Know - Share your expertise with others
  • Learn What You Don't - Acquire new skills from community members
  • No Money Involved - Pure skill-for-skill exchange
  • Community Driven - Built by learners, for learners

Safety Guidelines

For your protection, we recommend:

  1. Always meet in public places for in-person exchanges
  2. Verify user profiles before sharing personal information
  3. Use our messaging system for initial communications
  4. Report any suspicious activity to our team
